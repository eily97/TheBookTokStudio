Last updated: June 2026

thatpart ("we", "us") is a community for readers to share chapter-by-chapter reactions to books. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account info: when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We don't see or store your Google password.
  • Content you post: comments, replies, and chapter name suggestions you submit, along with the display name attached to them.
  • Usage data: basic, anonymized analytics (page views, general traffic) via Vercel Analytics — no individual tracking profiles.

How we use it

To run the core product: showing your comments to other readers, sending you notifications when someone replies to or likes your comment, and keeping your reading list. We don't sell your data, and we don't share it with advertisers.

Where it's stored

Data is stored with Supabase (database) and Vercel (hosting), both of which are industry-standard infrastructure providers with their own security practices.

Your choices

You can sign out at any time. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email us at thebooktokstudio@protonmail.com and we'll process it within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies

We use minimal local storage (not third-party tracking cookies) to keep you signed in and remember a few preferences like dismissed banners.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at thebooktokstudio@protonmail.com.

This is a general-purpose policy and not a substitute for legal advice. If thatpart grows significantly or starts handling more sensitive data, this should be reviewed by a lawyer familiar with applicable privacy law (e.g. GDPR/KVKK) for your specific situation.
